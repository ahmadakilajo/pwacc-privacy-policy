**Privacy Policy – PWACC (Piping Weight, Area & Cost Calculator)**

**Effective Date:** 2026

PWACC is developed by Ahmad Haidar Ahmad Akileh.

We respect your privacy. PWACC is an engineering application designed primarily for offline use. The application is available in both Free and Pro versions, which may differ in functionality and third-party services.

---

**1. Data Collection**

PWACC does not directly collect or store personal user information such as:

* Name
* Email address
* User-generated data

All engineering calculations are performed locally on the user's device.

https://policies.google.com/privacy

**3. Pro Version**

The Pro version of PWACC does not include advertisements and does not use third-party advertising services.

---

**4. Data Usage**

PWACC performs all calculations locally on the user's device. The application itself does not transmit or store calculation data externally.

**5. Data Sharing**

PWACC does not share personal data directly. Any data processing performed by third-party services (such as advertising providers in the Free version) is governed by their respective privacy policies.

Since PWACC does not store personal data, the risk of user data exposure from the application itself is minimized. Third-party services are responsible for the security of any data they process.
